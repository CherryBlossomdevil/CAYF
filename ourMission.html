China Africa special summit on unity and anti epidemic

President Xi Jinping delivered a keynote speech at the special summit on solidarity and epidemic prevention in June 17th. He stressed that China and Africa should unswervingly join hands to fight the epidemic, promote China Africa cooperation, jointly build a China Africa health community and promote the high-level development of China Africa comprehensive strategic cooperative partnership.

Central African community of common destiny

President Xi Jinping called on the thirty-third summit of the African Union in Addisababa, capital of Ethiopia, on February 9th to extend warm congratulations to African countries and peoples. Xi Jinping stressed that Sino African relations are at the best stage of history. Building a closer China Africa community of common destiny is in the common interests of the Chinese and African people.

China Africa Cooperation Forum

On October 12th, President Xi Jinping and President Sall of the non co chairmen of the China Africa Cooperation Forum, President of Senegal, made a congratulatory message on the establishment of the China Africa Cooperation Forum 20th anniversary. Xi Jinping and Sall pointed out in their congratulatory telegrams that China and Africa are willing to continue to uphold the spirit of solidarity and cooperation and jointly cope with all kinds of risks and challenges, so that China Africa cooperation will become a model of multilateralism and mutual benefit and win-win results.

China's international development cooperation in the new era

The Information Office of the State Council issued a white paper on China's international development cooperation in the new era on January 10.
