What We Do

Our mission is to mobilize African diasporan changemakers and their allies in Japan and equip them with resources to contribute to a prosperous and dignified Africa.

Networking
Events

Jaspora runs monthly networking events centered around various Afrocentric topics. Our events provide an excellent space to connect with members of the African diaspora in Japan as well as global allies interested in learning about and working with Africa.

Mentorship
Program

We pair highly motivated students and young professionals to mentors in Japan and across the globe over a 3-month period to transfer community knowledge within our network.

Workshops

Our workshops provide students and young professionals with 5 essential skills necessary for thriving in a rapidly changing world.

These skills are:

Problem Solving
Multicultural Understanding
Impactful Communication
Environmental Conscience
Team Building
